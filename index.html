About

I am a researcher and PhD candidate at the Federal University of ABC (UFABC), Brazil, affiliated with the Center for Engineering, Modeling and Applied Science (CECS). I am also a faculty member in the Department of Computer Engineering at Michael Okpara University of Agriculture Umudike, Nigeria.

My research spans Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT) security, Federated Learning, and Machine Learning for intrusion detection and privacy-preserving systems. I focus on developing lightweight, resource-aware intelligent models for securing IoT and edge computing environments.

I have over 532 citations and have published in leading venues including IEEE Access, Computers & Security, Expert Systems with Applications, Sustainability, and the International Symposium on Wireless Communication Systems (ISWCS). I collaborate with researchers across Brazil, Malaysia, Chile, Pakistan, and Nigeria.
